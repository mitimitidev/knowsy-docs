Technical risk register

WRO automated access
Westchester Records Online may block or rate-limit automated login and document retrieval. No published API; access via browser automation (Playwright). CAPTCHA is present on some flows.
High P0
OCR quality on historical deeds
Pre-1970 deeds are often typewritten on degraded paper. OCR confidence may drop below acceptable threshold, requiring manual review or re-scan. Volume of affected documents is unknown until tested at scale.
High P1
AI extraction schema drift
Deed language varies significantly across decades and attorneys. The Claude parsing prompt may need tuning for edge-case deed formats. A validation layer with human review fallback is required from day one.
Medium P1
Prior-deed chain termination
Digital records have a cutoff date (~1960 in WRO). The chain-walking loop must handle graceful termination and clearly communicate to users when the chain is incomplete.
Medium P2
Assessment roll data freshness
County assessment data is updated annually. Recent sales may not reflect the current owner of record. Report must include a data freshness disclaimer and date stamp.
Low P3

Implementation roadmap

Week 1–2

Foundation

Monorepo setup, Next.js App Router scaffold, Postgres schema, Vercel project. Stripe integration and checkout flow. Address search with assessment roll integration. Basic auth (email magic link for admin).

Week 3–4

Pipeline core

WRO automation (Playwright). Queue-based job runner. OCR integration (Google Document AI). Claude deed parsing with schema validation. Job status endpoint and polling UI.

Week 5

Chain walking & report assembly

Prior-deed chaining loop with max-depth guard. Report data model in Postgres. Report detail page rendering. PDF export. Email delivery.

Week 6

Reliability & QA

Failure states, auto-refund trigger, dead-letter queue alerts. End-to-end test with 20 real Westchester addresses. Performance benchmarking. Security review (payment data, PII handling).

Week 7

Beta launch

Private beta with 5–10 invited users. Feedback collection. Bug triage. Pricing validation.
