Deliverable 1 — Product definition document

Product vision

Knowsy is a consumer-facing web app that generates automated property title reports for Westchester County real estate. A buyer enters a street address, pays a flat fee, and receives a structured report containing the chain of ownership, recorded encumbrances, and AI-extracted red flags — without needing to hire a title company for preliminary research.

Primary user

Home buyers and real estate agents doing pre-purchase due diligence on residential properties in Westchester County, NY.

Core value proposition

Get the key facts about a property's ownership history in minutes, not days — at a fraction of the cost of a full title search.

Deliverable 2 — MVP scope & priorities

In scope — MVP

  • Address search and property lookup via Westchester assessment roll
  • Automated WRO deed retrieval for current owner
  • OCR extraction and AI parsing of deed text
  • Chain of title reconstruction (walk back through prior deeds)
  • Structured report: ownership timeline, encumbrances, flags
  • Stripe checkout — flat-fee per report
  • Email delivery of completed report (PDF or web link)

Out of scope — post-MVP

  • Counties outside Westchester
  • Commercial property
  • Lien searches beyond what is in recorded deeds
  • Attorney review or professional certification
  • User accounts / report history

Open questions & assumptions log

WRO access policy The Westchester Records Online portal may throttle or block automated access. The mitigation plan (Phase 3) covers alternative ingestion paths — including vendor APIs and bulk data licensing.
Assumption: single county for MVP We are scoping to Westchester County only. Expanding to additional counties (Nassau, Suffolk, Rockland) is a post-MVP milestone that will require evaluating each county's record access model separately.
Pricing model TBD Per-report flat fee is the working assumption. Subscription or credit-pack models remain on the table pending cost analysis (Phase 4).

Success metrics — MVP

  • Report successfully generated for ≥ 90% of valid Westchester addresses
  • End-to-end report time under 3 minutes
  • Deed extraction accuracy ≥ 95% on grantor/grantee/date fields
  • Cost per report (API + compute) under $2.00
