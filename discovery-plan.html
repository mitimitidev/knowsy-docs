Executive summary

The Discovery Phase is complete. We validated the core product concept, established a technical architecture built on Next.js and Vercel, and identified a recommended vendor stack for property data ingestion. The OCR and deed-extraction workflow has a clear implementation path. Wireframes were developed for the marketing landing page and the report detail page. The findings confirm the MVP is buildable. The primary risk — automating access to Westchester County land records via WRO — was mitigated by building a quick prototype that successfully extracted deed data, confirming the approach is viable. We recommend proceeding to the build phase.
